# Bocardo

## Open World Village Building M”MO”RPG

Skills like Fishing, Woodcutting, Farming and Mining etc. which are used to make better tools, make combat gear(?) and build the village as well as to do quests in the village. To get better resources you travel to new islands where the next resource is located or you travel to a procedurally generated land where the further away from “spawn” you get the better resources you find, these resources can be refined to be used in crafting and building.

## Skills

The skills that are planned so far are fishing, woodcutting, farming and mining. <br> <br>

### fishing

The fishing skill is based on beeing by water with a fishing rod and the fish you may catch are based on the rod and bait you are using as well as the biome you are currently fishing in.

#### Levels

The importaint levels should be the following: <br>

Level 1: Where you start of fishing for sardines with a fishing net, this yealds 15 xp and the sardines which are used as bait or to make oil.

Level 10: Where you can start to use a fishing rod to catch trout who yeald 25 xp, they are used for bait and to make food. You can also upgrade the fishing net and rod using the tool station.

Level 12: You start to earn more sardines per time you fish going as far as 5x at level 25, but this only yealds 15 + (5*editional sardines) xp.

Level 20: You can now upgrade your fishing rod to be able to catch salmon who yeald 40 xp and can be used to make better oil and better food, but they use sardines or worms as a bait.

Level 30: You can now craft cages to fish for lobsters which yealds 60 xp per lobbster and you can catch multiple but they use trout chunks as bait. They are used as food and to upgrade various things.

Level 40: You can now make Harpoon, upgrade the rod and the cage and catch swordfish who yeald 140 xp per, they are used in uppgrades.

Level 50: You can now fish sharks who yeald 200 xp and are used to make upgrades and oil, you can also make the gargantuin rod which is used at level 51 to catch whales.

Level 51: This is max level, now you can catch whales who give 300 xp and use them to make oil.

#### Tools

##### Nets

##### Bait

Trout chunks: Made from trout and are used when catching lobsters.

### Mining

The mining skill is based on hitting different rocks around the world with a pickaxe to get ore and other minerals.

#### Levels

The importaint levels should be the following: <br>

Level 1: Where you start of mining rocks with a chance to get copper and tin to make bronze to upgrade tools and build in the village. You also get rocks, you get 10 xp for rocks and 20 xp for tin and copper.

Level 10: You can now start to mine iron ore which yealds 25 xp and can be used to upgrade tools and build in the village. You can also upgrade the pickaxe.

Level 20: You can now upgrade your pickaxe to be able to mine coal which yeald 40 xp and can be used with iron to make steel.

Level 30: You can now mine silver to get 80 xp, you can also start to get jewels from all rocks.

Level 40: You can now mine Gold to get 140 xp and you can upgrade the pickaxe.

Level 50: You can now mine Gem rocks to get 200 xp and various gems to upgrade the town and tools and you can upgrade the pickaxe.

Level 51: This is max level, you can now mine Bocardite to get 300 xp and to make the best gear.

#### Tools
